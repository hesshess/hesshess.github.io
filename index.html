만들고 싶은게 생겼다

Hess · Survivor

After deciding to become a developer, there was one piece of advice that always lingered like a sense of guilt:
“Learn by building the app you want to create!”
Self-directed learning—it’s such an obvious and effective approach, almost like saying, “If you want to learn a foreign language, date a native speaker!”
But who doesn’t know that? The real problem is being unable to put it into action.

From the moment I first started coding until recently, I never once imagined creating an app I genuinely wanted to build.
I was always too busy mimicking what others were making.
And maybe that’s why I’ve always been just an mediocre developer.

Then one day, I suddenly had this thought:
Waiting to land a job as an old mediocre developer in a struggling economy isn’t very inspiring.
The person I imagine myself to be isn’t this dull.
So why have I let myself live this way for 35 years, settling into being just another ordinary person?

I wasn’t born into a wealthy family, I didn’t graduate from a prestigious university, I don’t have a big savings account, and I don’t stand out in appearance.
But if there’s one thing I’ve always been proud of, it’s my sense of “cool.”
I didn’t want to do anything that felt uncool, and I refused to do it.
That’s what allowed me to be confident in myself, even without anything else to boast about.
(By “uncool,” I mean the broader definition of what I personally perceive as cool, not something related to appearances or visuals)

Of course, this confidence only applied to me internally.
Externally, in society, I’m just a broke nobody, a braggart.
It’s sad, but it’s the reality, and I accept it.

So what do I do now?
Am I going to sit here and sink even deeper?
Am I going to become a burden to my family?
(That’s the absolute worst-case scenario.)
No, I can’t let it end like this.
My family’s legacy isn’t wealth or fame; it’s survival.
Staying alive, enduring—that’s our inheritance.

So I thought long and hard about what I can do.
And for the first time in five years, I suddenly found something I want to create.
